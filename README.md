# 💧 Journey-of-Wastewater-ETP-STP-and-PCB-Compliance.
     
A visual explainer project that demonstrates the industrial wastewater treatment process using image-based workflows, short AI voiceovers, and infographic storytelling.

This project explains how Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP) and Sewage Treatment Plants (STP) help industries and residential facilities treat wastewater and comply with environmental regulations from the Pollution Control Board (PCB).

The goal is to create a simple, visual, and educational representation of water treatment systems used in real-world industrial environments.

# 📌 Project Objective

Industrial and residential wastewater can cause serious environmental damage if discharged untreated.

This project visually explains:

• How industrial wastewater is treated using Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP)
• How domestic sewage is treated using Sewage Treatment Plants (STP)
• How industries comply with Pollution Control Board regulations
• How treated water contributes to environmental sustainability

The workflow is designed as a short visual explainer with AI-generated visuals and voiceover narration.

# 🎬 Workflow Overview

The video is divided into 6 scenes, each explaining a key stage of wastewater management.

1️⃣ Water Pollution Problem

Industrial processes generate wastewater containing chemicals and pollutants.
Untreated discharge can contaminate rivers, soil, and ecosystems.

2️⃣ Wastewater Collection

Wastewater from factories is collected through pipelines and directed into treatment systems for processing.

3️⃣ Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP)

Industrial wastewater undergoes multiple treatment stages including:

1. Equalization

2. Chemical treatment

3. Biological treatment

4. Clarification and filtration

These processes remove harmful pollutants and contaminants.

4️⃣ Sewage Treatment Plant (STP)

Domestic sewage from residential buildings and commercial facilities is treated through biological processes to produce reusable water.

5️⃣ Pollution Control Board Compliance

Industries must obtain environmental approvals before operating.

Key approvals include:

1. Consent to Establish (CTE)

2. Consent to Operate (CTO)

These approvals ensure industries follow environmental regulations.

6️⃣ Clean Water and Sustainability

After treatment, water can be safely reused for applications such as:

1. Gardening

2. Industrial cooling

3. Flushing systems

This helps conserve freshwater resources and protect the environment.
